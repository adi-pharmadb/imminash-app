# Product Mission

## Pitch

Imminash is an AI-powered Australian migration intelligence platform that helps skilled visa applicants navigate the PR process -- from occupation matching and points estimation to skill assessment document preparation -- replacing the $1,200+ migration agent workflow with an automated, human-vetted experience at a fraction of the cost.

## Users

### Primary Customers

- **Skilled Visa Applicants**: People applying for Australian PR through skilled pathways (189, 190, 491) who need to understand which occupation fits their profile, estimate their points, and prepare skill assessment documents
- **Temporary Visa Holders**: People on student, work, or bridging visas exploring PR pathways and needing clarity on their options and timelines
- **Offshore Applicants**: Skilled workers overseas considering Australian migration who need to assess their eligibility before committing time and money

### Secondary Users

- **Migration Agents**: Who may use the tool to triage and pre-qualify clients faster
- **Internal Case Officers**: Who review AI-generated documents and manage the human-in-the-loop workflow

## The Problem

### Fragmented, Confusing Information

Australian migration involves 1,000+ ANZSCO occupation codes spread across multiple government lists (MLTSSL, STSOL, CSOL, ROL). Each occupation has a different assessing body, different visa eligibility, and different state nomination availability. Most applicants don't know where to start.

**Our Solution:** A single flow that matches the user's profile to the best-fit occupations, calculates their points, and maps out realistic visa pathways across all states.

### Expensive, Slow Skill Assessment Preparation

Migration agents charge $1,200-$1,500 AUD to prepare skill assessment documents. The work is largely templated -- aligning job duties and qualifications with what the assessing body expects -- but people pay because they don't want to risk a negative assessment.

**Our Solution:** AI-powered document generation that aligns the user's profile with assessing body requirements, with optional human review by a registered migration agent, at a fraction of the traditional cost.

### No Momentum, No Guidance

Most young migrants procrastinate because the process feels overwhelming. There's no tool that guides them step-by-step from "I want PR" to "my skill assessment is submitted."

**Our Solution:** A guided, momentum-driven experience that takes users from profile input to document-ready in days instead of months.

## Differentiators

### AI + Official Data (Not One or the Other)

AI is used only where it adds value (occupation matching, document generation). All critical outputs -- points calculation, pathway signals, state eligibility, list status -- are rule-based and driven by official government data. No hallucinated advice.

### Human-in-the-Loop Where It Matters

Documents are AI-generated but can be reviewed by a registered migration agent. This gives users the confidence of human oversight at software-level margins.

### Full Journey, Not Just a Calculator

Unlike basic points calculators scattered across migration agent websites, Imminash covers the full pre-visa journey: occupation selection, points estimation, pathway mapping, and skill assessment document preparation.

## Key Features

### Phase 1: Migration Intelligence (Current -- Legacy Tool)

- **Occupation Matching**: AI-powered matching of user profiles to ANZSCO codes with fallback keyword matching
- **Points Calculator**: Rule-based DHA points estimation across 10 categories with proper caps
- **Pathway Mapping**: Visa eligibility signals for 189/190/491/482/186 based on list status and experience
- **State Nomination Matrix**: Eligibility across all 8 states/territories using official state occupation lists
- **189 Invitation Data**: Historical minimum points thresholds to contextualize the user's position

### Phase 2: Skill Assessment Preparation (Next -- The Revenue Product)

- **Assessing Body Requirements**: Pull specific criteria for the user's target occupation and assessing body
- **Document Generation**: AI-generated employment references, duty statements, and supporting documents aligned with assessing body expectations
- **Gap Analysis**: Identify where the user's profile falls short and recommend actions
- **Human Review**: Optional registered migration agent review of AI-generated documents
- **Submission Guidance**: Step-by-step instructions for submitting to the assessing body

## Value Proposition

For $200-$800 (vs $1,200-$1,500 with a migration agent), users get:
- Clarity on their best-fit occupation in minutes
- Accurate points estimation with pathway signals
- AI-generated skill assessment documents aligned with their assessing body's requirements
- Optional human review for confidence
- Guided momentum from start to submission
